Our achievements at Mount View

Two-time International School Award

Mount View School (MVS) is an ISO 9001 - Certified Grade “A” school with a two-time International School Award (ISA) winner by the British Council established in B.S 2048 Mangsir 20th (6th December 1991) in Changathali, Lalitpur, with 63 students by the late founder principal Raj Kumar Maharjan. In B.S. 2050, a new branch in Balkot was opened, but later on, in B.S. 2054, both Changathali and Balkot were merged and situated in Balkot, Bhaktapur

First School to implement SQC in Their Cirriculum

Mount View School was the first school in Nepal to implement SQC(Students' Quality Circle) in their cirriculum as a compulsory subject. SQC produces a pratical approcach to life including various philosophical ideas and methods to make studying more efficient. Our students have took this approach and we got various improvement in studies across a bunch of students. Our students also reported improved discipline, continous improvement in stuies and a lotof other self improvements.
