Mount View School

About Mount View School

Mount View School is a caring and progressive learning community where students grow academically, socially, and emotionally. We believe in creating a supportive environment where every child can explore their strengths and build confidence for the future.

Our Facilities at Mount View

Sports and Extracirricular Facililties

At Mount View School, we believe that physical excellence goes hand in hand with academic success. Our state-of-the-art sports facilities are designed to inspire every student to move, compete, and thrive. From our pristine, full-sized athletic fields and premium indoor courts to our modern gymnastics zones and swimming pools, we provide an elite environment for both recreational play and competitive sports. Backed by expert coaching staff and top-tier training equipment, Mount View School offers the ultimate launchpad for young athletes to build teamwork, discipline, and a lifelong passion for fitness.

Science Facilities

At Mount View School, we believe that innovation and discovery begin with hands-on experience. Our state-of-the-art science laboratory facilities are designed to transform theoretical concepts into real-world insights, inspiring the next generation of researchers and innovators. Equipped with advanced technology, modern safety systems, and university-grade apparatus across dedicated physics, chemistry, and biology bays, students are empowered to experiment, analyze, and problem-solve in a professional setting. Guided by expert faculty, Mount View School provides an elite environment where curiosity is nurtured, critical thinking is sharpened, and a lifelong passion for scientific inquiry is born.

Child Development

At Mount View School, we believe that the foundation for a lifetime of success is built during a child's earliest years. Our dedicated child development and early learning facilities are carefully designed to nurture the cognitive, emotional, and social growth of our youngest learners. Through bright, collaborative spaces, sensory-rich play areas, and interactive learning environments, we encourage students to explore, create, and build essential confidence. Guided by specialist educators who understand the unique milestones of early childhood, Mount View School provides a safe, stimulating, and joyful environment where curiosity is sparked and a foundational love for learning begins.

Advanced Programming Classes

At Mount View School, we believe that digital literacy is a fundamental tool for shaping the future. Our cutting-edge computer laboratories and technology classrooms are designed to equip students with the essential skills needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving digital world. Featuring high-performance workstations, high-speed connectivity, and the latest software suites, our facilities provide the perfect ecosystem for learning everything from fundamental digital literacy and graphic design to advanced coding, robotics, and data analysis. Under the guidance of expert tech instructors, Mount View School ensures that every student transforms from a passive consumer of technology into an innovative creator and confident digital citizen.

School Library

At Mount View School, we believe that the library is the intellectual heart of our campus, serving as a sanctuary for imagination, research, and quiet reflection. Our modern library facility is designed to foster a deep, lifelong love for reading and independent inquiry. Boasting an expansive collection that spans classic literature, contemporary fiction, and extensive academic journals, alongside seamless access to global digital archives, students have an unparalleled world of information at their fingertips. Featuring collaborative group study zones, cozy independent reading nooks, and the guidance of dedicated literacy specialists, Mount View School provides an inspiring space where curiosity is sparked, knowledge is deepened, and minds are expanded.
